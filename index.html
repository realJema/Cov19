Tracking Coronavirus COVID-19

The first case of the new Coronavirus COVID-19 was recorded on 31 December in Wuhan, China (WHO). Since then, 197,145 confirmed cases have been recorded worldwide. This visualization shows the near real-time status based on data from the Center for Systems Science and Engineering (CSSE) at Johns Hopkins University and DXY. Data currently available on the following zoom levels: City level - US, Canada and Australia; Province level - China; Country level - other countries. Learn how this map was created.
